This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Overview

GRM Spiritual Library provides access to religious books, hymns, and media. We prioritize minimal data collection and transparent handling of any information you provide.

What we collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the app:

  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve features
  • Push notification tokens (optional)
  • Content metadata (titles, authors, hymn names)

What we do not do

We do not sell personal data, and we do not perform ad‑tracking or profiling for advertising purposes.

Images and media

Sample pictures and thumbnails are hosted on our servers or trusted CDNs. We do not extract personal metadata from user images unless you explicitly upload content and consent to processing.

Notifications

Notifications are optional. You can opt in or out in device settings. Tokens are used only to deliver messages and are not linked to personal profiles.

Security

We use industry standard safeguards to protect data. While we strive to keep data secure, no system is completely immune to breaches. If you suspect an issue, contact us immediately.

Children

If your app targets children under 13, we require parental consent and only collect data strictly necessary for the app’s operation. Contact us for details on parental controls and data deletion requests.

Contact and requests

If you have questions, want data removed, or need more details, email rowen@example.com or use the app’s contact form.
